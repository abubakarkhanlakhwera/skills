## How to Update This File
When you modify SKILL.md or INSTRUCTIONS.md:
1. Bump the version (patch for small tweaks, minor for new sections, major for philosophy changes)
2. Add an entry below describing what changed and why
3. Commit with message: `skill(flutter): [description]`

## Version Format
```
MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH

MAJOR — fundamental change to AI behaviour or philosophy
MINOR — new section or significant rule added
PATCH — clarification, typo fix, or minor rule tweak
```
